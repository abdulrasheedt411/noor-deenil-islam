Understanding Islam

The Message of Peace, Justice, and Pure Monotheism (Tawheed). Islam as revealed in the Quran and taught by Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

What is Islam?

Islam is not a new religion but the same eternal message revealed through all prophets including Adam, Noah, Abraham, Moses, Jesus, and finally Muhammad (peace be upon them all). The word "Islam" means submission to the will of Allah and comes from the same root as "Salaam" - meaning peace.

Islam teaches that all human beings are born pure and are accountable directly to Allah. It provides a complete way of life covering spiritual, social, economic, and political aspects based on the Quran and the example of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

"Indeed, the religion in the sight of Allah is Islam." - Quran 3:19

Quick Facts About Islam

  • Followers: 1.9+ billion Muslims worldwide
  • Meaning: "Submission to the will of Allah"
  • Holy Book: The Quran (preserved in original Arabic)
  • Final Prophet: Muhammad (peace be upon him)
  • Place of Worship: Mosque (Masjid)
  • Core Belief: Tawheed (Oneness of Allah)

Jesus Taught Islam - Not Christianity

Jesus (peace be upon him) and his true disciples were not "Christians" - they were "Muslims" (submitters to God's will).

Jesus' Core Message

"Not everyone who says to me, 'Lord, Lord,' will enter the kingdom of heaven, but only the one who does the will of my Father who is in heaven."

Matthew 7:21

Jesus defined his true followers as those who DO GOD'S WILL, not those who merely call him "Lord."

Islam Means Submission

The word "Islam" means submission to God's will. A "Muslim" is one who submits to God's will.

Therefore: When Jesus said his true followers are those who "do the will of my Father," he was describing Muslims - not "Christians" as understood today.

Jesus and his disciples never called themselves "Christians" - that term came later in Antioch (Acts 11:26).

We Are Jesus' Brothers

"For whoever does the will of my Father in heaven is my brother and sister and mother."

Matthew 12:50

According to Jesus' own words, Muslims are his true brothers and sisters because we strive to do God's will as revealed in the Quran.

Muslims follow the same God (Allah), believe in all the prophets including Jesus, and strive to submit to God's will.

The Key Understanding

Christianity (as practiced today) = Following Paul's teachings about Jesus
Islam = Following Jesus' ACTUAL teachings about submitting to God's will

Muslims are the true spiritual brothers and sisters of Jesus (peace be upon him) because we follow his core message: Submit to God's will.

Core Islamic Beliefs (Articles of Faith)

Islam is built upon six fundamental beliefs that every Muslim must accept.

1. Belief in Allah

Tawheed: Belief in the absolute Oneness of Allah. He has no partners, no children, no equals. He is the Creator, Sustainer, and Only One worthy of worship.

2. Belief in Angels

Angels are created from light who constantly worship Allah and carry out His commands. They include Jibreel (Gabriel), Mikael, Israfil, and the recording angels.

3. Belief in Divine Books

Allah revealed scriptures including the Torah (Tawrat), Psalms (Zabur), Gospel (Injeel), and finally the Quran - the only perfectly preserved scripture.

4. Belief in Prophets

Allah sent prophets to guide humanity, beginning with Adam and ending with Muhammad (peace be upon them all). All taught the same message of Tawheed.

5. Belief in Day of Judgment

This world will end, all beings will be resurrected, and judged according to their beliefs and deeds. Eternal paradise or hell awaits based on this judgment.

6. Belief in Divine Decree

Allah knows everything that will happen, and everything occurs by His will. However, humans have free will and are accountable for their choices.
