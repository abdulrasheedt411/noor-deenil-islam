About the Founder

Abdul Rasheed Nkhoma

Hafiz al-Quran Science Student

Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh,

I am Abdul Rasheed Nkhoma, founder of Noor Deenil Islam. Born and raised in Chasefu, I completed my Islamic education at Lundazi Islamic Institute (2014-2023), memorizing the entire Quran under Maulana Zaheer. Currently, I'm a 3rd-year Science Education student at Makeni College of Education.

6+ Years of Dialogue Experience

My journey in Christian-Muslim dialogue began in 2018 when I was in Grade 9. For 6 years, I have actively engaged in discussions, debates, and comparative religion studies. This extensive experience gives me deep insight into common questions, biblical arguments, and effective approaches for presenting Islam with wisdom and evidence.

This unique combination of Quranic memorization, scientific education, and practical dialogue experience enables me to present Islam through multiple lenses: spiritual, logical, and evidence-based.
