About the Founder

Abdul Rasheed Nkhoma

Hafiz al-Quran
Science Education Student

Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh,

I am Abdul Rasheed Nkhoma, founder of Noor Deenil Islam. Born and raised in Chasefu, I completed my Islamic education at Lundazi Islamic Institute from 2014 to 2023, where I was blessed to memorize the entire Quran (Hifz) under the guidance of Maulana Zaheer.

Currently, I am in my third year pursuing Science Education at Makeni College of Education. This combination of traditional Islamic knowledge and modern scientific education gives me a unique perspective in presenting Islam through both spiritual understanding and logical reasoning.

This website represents my commitment to Dawah—inviting people to Islam with wisdom, beautiful preaching, and evidence-based dialogue. As a Hafiz, I have a deep connection with the Quran, and as a science student, I appreciate the importance of logic and evidence in discussions.

"My mission is to present Islam as the complete way of life—perfectly compatible with reason, science, and human nature. Through respectful dialogue with Christians and followers of other faiths, I aim to clarify misconceptions and present the beautiful message of Tawheed."
