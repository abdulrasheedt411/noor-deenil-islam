My Journey in Dawah

Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatuh,

I am Abdul Rasheed Nkhoma, founder of Noor Deenil Islam. Born and raised in Chasefu, I completed my Islamic education at Lundazi Islamic Institute from 2014 to 2023, where I was blessed to memorize the entire Quran (Hifz) under the guidance of Maulana Zaheer.

Currently, I am in my third year pursuing Science Education at Makeni College of Education. This combination of traditional Islamic knowledge and modern scientific education gives me a unique perspective in presenting Islam through both spiritual understanding and logical reasoning.

Dialogue Experience Since 2018

My journey in Christian-Muslim dialogue began in 2018 when I was in Grade 9. For 6 years, I have actively engaged in discussions, debates, and comparative religion studies. This extensive experience has given me deep insight into common questions, biblical arguments, and effective approaches for presenting Islam with wisdom and evidence.

This unique combination of Quranic memorization, scientific education, and practical dialogue experience enables me to present Islam through multiple lenses: spiritual, logical, and evidence-based.
