Our Mission in Interfaith Dialogue

At Noor Deenil Islam, we believe in following the Quranic injunction: "Invite to the way of your Lord with wisdom and beautiful preaching, and argue with them in a way that is best." (Quran 16:125)

Our dialogue platform is built on three foundational pillars that guide every discussion:

Knowledge-Based

Using evidence from the Quran, authentic Hadith, Biblical scriptures, historical facts, and logical reasoning.

Respectful Character

Maintaining the prophetic example of beautiful character, patience, and avoiding personal attacks.

Truth-Seeking

Focusing on discovering truth rather than winning arguments, with sincerity in intentions.

Key Discussion Topics

We focus on core theological and philosophical topics that form the basis of Islamic-Christian dialogue:

The Nature of God

Islamic Tawheed: Absolute Oneness of Allah, no partners, no sons, no equals.

Christian Trinity: Father, Son, Holy Spirit as three persons in one God.

Discussion: Biblical vs Quranic concept of God, logical coherence, historical development.

Jesus in Islam & Christianity

Islam: Prophet Isa (Jesus), born miraculously to Virgin Mary, not divine, not crucified.

Christianity: Divine Son of God, crucified for salvation, resurrected.

Discussion: Historical Jesus, crucifixion accounts, second coming prophecies.

Scripture Authenticity

Quran: Preserved letter-by-letter, unchanged since revelation.

Bible: Multiple versions, textual variants, historical compilation.

Discussion: Preservation methods, manuscript evidence, contradictions.

Salvation & Afterlife

Islam: Salvation through faith + good deeds, accountability before Allah.

Christianity: Salvation through faith in Jesus' sacrifice.

Discussion: Concept of sin, forgiveness, heaven/hell, divine justice.

Rules of Engagement

1
Respect is Mandatory

All participants must be treated with dignity regardless of their beliefs. No insults, mockery, or demeaning language.

2
Evidence-Based Discussion

Support claims with sources: Quranic verses, authentic Hadith, Biblical references, historical facts, or logical reasoning.

3
Good Character (Akhlaq)

Follow the prophetic example: patience, wisdom, beautiful speech, and controlling anger.

4
Clarity Over Victory

Aim for understanding and truth-discovery, not just winning arguments or scoring points.

5
Sincerity in Intentions

Approach discussions with sincere desire to understand, clarify, and seek truth—not to embarrass others.
